Take the guesswork out of meeting costs. With the Calendyze Chrome plug-in extension, your team knows the exact financial impact of every meeting before it's even scheduled … making it easier than ever to determine whether each meeting is a smart investment or a potential drain on your bottom line.

Cut Costs, Not Corners: Optimize Meeting Spending

Trust Calendyze to Protect Your Privacy and Security

At Calendyze, we recognize that your privacy isn't just important—it's paramount. That's why we deploy the most advanced safeguards to ensure your calendar and financial details remain 100% secure.

We Offer Ironclad Protection for Your Sensitive Data

To ensure the security of your calendar and financial data, we use top-tier encryption and secure OAuth. We never view or store any of your team’s Google passwords. You have complete control and can cut off access.

We Don’t Keep Your Data Hanging Around

Calendyze respects your privacy by not storing any of your data longer than necessary. Your calendar and financial data are used strictly for optimizing your experience. We don’t snoop through your meeting details, and we never sell or monetize your data.

Just the Access We Need

Calendyze operates on minimal permissions, requesting only the essential access needed to deliver its functionality. Our focused approach guarantees a secure environment for all of your confidential financial and calendar data.
